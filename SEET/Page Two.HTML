Where is the CKB located?

    Located on the corner of New St. and Dr. MLK Jr. Blvd.

Where can I study in the CKB?

    On the 3rd floor (up the stairs entering the front of the building), there is a quiet study room to the left. Throughout the upper floors, there are also tables and seats to study around. As well as breakout rooms, which you can reserve

I want to study with a group, how can I do that?

    NJIT offers Breakout Rooms in the CKB. For questions on how to reserve here.

I need help with my classes, where can I get a tutor?

    Just like the CKB's study room reservations, there are dedicated tutoring centers located in the Ground Floor of the CKB. Reservations can be made through there, or online. All tutoring centers on NJIT campus can be found here.
