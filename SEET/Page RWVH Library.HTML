Where can I study in the VHL?

    There are 4 floors in the building that offers a ton of open and private individual spaces to study.
  • Basement Floor: The main library starts here, over 60 private singular desks so no one can distract you from work.
  • 1st Floor: A whole floor with over 101 seats with monitors and a desktop in order to work on anything required desktop.
  • 2nd/3rd Floor: A mix of private singular desks, small group tables, big group tables, to maximise full groupwork and individual work efficiency.
