What is the Hillier College of Architecture and Design buidling?

    The HCAD building is a southward extension of Weston hall, along Martin Lurther King Jr. Boulevard which is the current building for the College of Architecture and Design.

How can I access the HCAD building?

    You can access the building from the campus by using an elevated ramp from Summit Place. You can also enter staright from Martin Lurther King Jr. Boulevard using your NJIT key card.

Where can I study in the HCAD building.

    Students can study at the Barbara and Leonard Littman Architecture and Design Library, located on the fourth floor of the building

What services does the library provide?

    The library provides traditional printing services, online and computural resources and also a wide selection of books.

What seating is availabe at the library?

    Desk and chairs and lounge chairs are available for seating.
