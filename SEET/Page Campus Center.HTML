Introduction:

    Located right by the Tech Cafe, Taco Bell, and other on-campus dining facilities, as well as the background buzz of other students, the Campus Center is a great place to study.



Where is the Campus Center?

    Located right in front of Fenster Hall, to the left of Kupfrian Hall, right by the NJIT clock.

Where could I study in the Campus Center?

  • First Floor by the Dining Area
      • There's a few seats and sofas in the center of the building. There are also a lot of outlets conveniently located in tables or by seating areas if needed.
  • Second Floor by Ballrooms
      • Around the many rooms on the 2nd floor, there are tables, booth seats, and lots of outlets to accompany the large amount of seats here. This space is mostly recognized as a lounge space, as many groups of people gather here. It's an ideal space for group studies and getaways.
  • Third Floor
      • The bridge between the Campus Center and Fenster Hall is another relaxing place to study, with a stellar view overlooking a large portion of the NJIT campus. It's an eye-pleasing and aesthetic environment apposed to the bustling movement of people in the Campus Center. It's more reserved and quieter, but it is still a very public space that doesn't prioritize silence.
      Another great feature of the 3rd floor is the Highlander Pub. It is mostly empty on mornings and weekday afternoons, so enjoying a nice meal as well as taking a moment to study/relax there is possible.

Important Reminder:

    The Campus Center is a shared community space dedicated as an area for ALL NJIT students. Please do not abuse the privilege that comes with studying in the Campus Center. Be respectful of the space and people around you.
